About Us

Rooted in Excellence. Growing with Purpose.

Since our founding in 2000, Vidyashree Shikshana Kendra has stood as a pillar of premium quality education and character development.

Our experienced faculty nurtures every student with care, blending academic rigor with a deep respect for community and culture.

We believe that true learning happens both inside and outside the classroom. That’s why we champion extracurricular excellence—from competitive sports to strategic chess.

Vidyashree is more than a school—it’s a home for growth, pride, and lifelong inspiration.
